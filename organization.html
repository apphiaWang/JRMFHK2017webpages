Dr. TAN Chee Wei Director

City University of Hong Kong

Biography: Chee Wei Tan received the M.A. and Ph.D. degrees from Princeton University in 2008. He founded the The Algebra Game Project sponsored by Facebook FbStart. His Erdos number is 3. He is interested in personalized learning technologies, computer science education and mathematics.

Mr. Shen Jian Co-Director

Sapiens Technologies

Biography: Shen Jian is a Partner at Sapiens Technologies in Hong Kong. He received his degree in Mathematics from Princeton University in 2007 and he was the president of the Princeton University Math Club. He has previously worked at Goldman Sachs.







Math Circle Mentor


Dr. Kenneth Shum

Chinese University of Hong Kong

Biography: Kenneth Shum received the B.Eng. degree in Information Engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong in 1993, and the M.S. and Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from University of Southern California in 1995 and 2000 respectively. He is now a research fellow in the Institute of Network Coding, CUHK. His research interests include information theory, coding theory and cooperative communication in wireless network. He is also a member of Composers and Authors Socity of Hong Kong (CASH) and has Erdos number 2.
